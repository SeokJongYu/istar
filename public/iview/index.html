iview

an interactive WebGL visualizer of protein-ligand complex

Your browser does not support WebGL.

iview is based on GLmol and supports the following keyrings

  • Left button: rotate the complex
  • Right button: translate the complex
  • Middle button / wheel: zoom in/out the complex
  • Shift + right button: change slab of the complex
